CHI2012

I had the pleasure to participate in CHI2012 in Texas. I just want to share a few things here.

I participated as a part of the exhibition with my Touchbox - You can find the article here. Jonas Löwgren, my supervisor, did a good job describing the trip from his point of view and how he sees CHI as one who has been a part of it from the beginning. You can read his report here. I took the time to document both the CHI conference, the exhibition and a little part of Texas. You can find the pictures here.

Of the many inspirational talks I would like to mention one here. It was the talk of Gaver about what to expect of research through design. You can find the paper here. At the exhibition the Touché Sensing was the most interesting piece (from a technology point of view). It was clear that the possibilities within touch and intimate touch interaction could be expanded with this technology. Right after the conference I asked DZL if we could build a version for Arduino. He managed to do it and you can read about it here.
