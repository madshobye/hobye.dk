Color control

Roskilde Festival (July 2015)

I helped make the entrance markers for Roskilde Festival 2015 interactive - the audience can change the colour of the LED lights in them by visiting rf.illutron.dk with their smartphone.

The project is a part of a larger strategy at Fablab RUC. Internet of things (IOT) has become a new technology platform to experiment and explore. Via IOT we are able make interactions that spans accross the physical and virtual reals.
